EY FAAS IPO Readiness Advisory

Completed EY Financial Accounting Advisory Services (FAAS) Job Simulation focused on IPO readiness strategy, governance enhancement, ESG compliance, financial reporting alignment, and investor communication advisory for a global fintech company.

Project Overview

Developed a structured IPO readiness proposal for a fast-growing fintech company preparing for public listing expansion across international markets.

The project focused on helping management strengthen financial reporting processes, governance structures, internal controls, ESG reporting frameworks, and stakeholder communication strategies to meet public market expectations.

The advisory framework simulated real-world FAAS consulting responsibilities involved during IPO preparation and regulatory readiness engagements.

Business Background

The fintech company operated across multiple geographies including the U.S., Europe, and India with strong customer growth and aggressive expansion plans through acquisitions and capital raising initiatives.

The company planned to pursue an Initial Public Offering (IPO) to expand operations, strengthen investor confidence, and enhance long-term market positioning.

Key Challenges Identified

  • Transitioning financial statements toward IFRS-compliant reporting standards
  • Improving governance structures including board independence and audit committee effectiveness
  • Designing stronger internal control and SOX-compliance frameworks
  • Enhancing ESG disclosure transparency and sustainability reporting practices
  • Aligning accounting policies with public company reporting expectations
  • Developing effective investor communication and IPO roadshow strategies
  • Managing valuation readiness and regulatory compliance risks

IPO Readiness Strategy

Built a phased IPO readiness framework covering pre-IPO planning, regulatory filing preparation, investor communication, governance restructuring, and post-listing compliance monitoring.

The strategy included financial audit readiness, accounting policy alignment, ESG reporting support, investor sentiment management, and milestone tracking for successful IPO execution.

Risk Assessment & Governance Advisory

  • Identified governance gaps and recommended board-level policy upgrades
  • Analysed regulatory and compliance risks impacting IPO timelines
  • Suggested internal control enhancements for financial reporting reliability
  • Designed mitigation strategies for ESG scrutiny and investor confidence concerns
  • Recommended stakeholder communication improvements for transparency

Skills & Concepts Applied

Investment Banking IPO Advisory FAAS Financial Reporting IFRS ESG Reporting Governance Risk Analysis Internal Controls Stakeholder Communication Process Improvement Commercial Awareness

Key Learnings

Strengthened practical understanding of IPO readiness processes, governance frameworks, financial reporting standards, and strategic advisory work performed by Financial Accounting Advisory Services teams.

The project also improved commercial awareness regarding investor expectations, ESG integration, regulatory preparedness, and operational scalability considerations during capital market transactions.
