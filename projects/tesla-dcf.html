Valuation & Financial Modelling

Tesla DCF Valuation Model

Developed a comprehensive discounted cash flow valuation model analysing Tesla’s long-term growth potential, operating leverage, capital efficiency, and terminal value assumptions.

Project Overview

This project involved building an integrated DCF valuation model to estimate Tesla’s intrinsic enterprise value based on projected future cash flows and long-term profitability assumptions.

The model focused on analysing revenue growth trajectories, margin expansion potential, reinvestment requirements, and sensitivity across multiple valuation drivers.

Key Assumptions

  • Revenue growth driven by EV adoption and energy business expansion
  • Operating margin improvements through scale efficiencies
  • Terminal growth and WACC sensitivity analysis
  • Capex and working capital forecasting assumptions
  • Scenario analysis for bull, base, and bear cases

Skills & Tools Used

DCF Valuation Financial Modelling Forecasting Excel Equity Research Sensitivity Analysis

Key Learnings

Strengthened understanding of valuation methodologies, forecasting frameworks, cost of capital estimation, and scenario-driven investment analysis.

The project also improved analytical thinking regarding high-growth technology businesses and long-duration valuation models.
