A Story of a Glass of Water

A reflective piece exploring life, ambition, survival, money, generational pressure, and the endless cycle of human desires through the simple metaphor of a glass of water.

💧 Here I wrote something from my heart…
A simple glass of water, and suddenly, the story of life unfolds.

𝐀 𝐒𝐭𝐨𝐫𝐲 𝐨𝐟 𝐚 𝐆𝐥𝐚𝐬𝐬 𝐨𝐟 𝐖𝐚𝐭𝐞𝐫

Kitna simple hota hai…
Take a glass of water…
to satisfy thirst.

But life—gets complicated.
When you want RO water, not just water.
Aur wahin se… life start hoti hai.

For RO water—you need machine.
For machine—you need money.
For money—you need job, or business.
For that—you need degree.
For that—you need better education.
For that—you need good parents’ income.
For that—you need parents.
For that—parents need money.

And the other generation start…
when parents need money.

For that… you need another glass of water.

𝐃𝐞𝐤𝐡𝐚?
Ek glass water ko peene ke liye…
ek full life, ek generation chali gayi.

𝐑𝐞𝐬𝐮𝐥𝐭: no one satisfy.
Abhi pyaas nahi bhuji.

Life is too short…
too long.

Bas jeeyo.
No matter what you have…or not.
